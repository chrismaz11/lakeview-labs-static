Automation is evolving into intelligence

Automation is evolving into intelligence

The shift from rule-based workflows to adaptive systems

Tobias Klein

·

March 24, 2026

Automation used to be simple.

Define a rule. Trigger an action. Repeat.

This worked when systems were predictable. But modern workflows are not static — they change, evolve, and grow more complex over time.

Static automation can’t keep up.

What teams need now is something different.

Not just automation — but intelligence.

With AI-powered workflows, systems can adapt. They can make decisions, adjust to new inputs, and improve over time.

This is the next phase of automation.

And it changes how work gets done entirely.
