“This isn’t just automation; it’s true operational intelligence. Lake View Labs has completely redefined the standard for our industry’s workflow engine.”

Aiko Tanaka
Director, Speed-to-Lead

Industry

AI & Data Platforms

Team size

90+ employees

Use case

Intelligent workflows

Overview

Speed-to-Lead had already implemented automation across key parts of their operations, but found that traditional systems lacked flexibility and adaptability.

As their business evolved, workflows became more complex and required a more intelligent approach to decision-making.

The Challenge

Rule-based automation systems proved too rigid for dynamic environments.

Workflows could not adapt to changing conditions, and updates required manual adjustments. This limited the team’s ability to scale operations efficiently and respond to new challenges.

The need for more adaptive, intelligent systems became clear.

The Approach

Speed-to-Lead implemented Lake View Labs to introduce AI-driven decision-making into their workflows.



Instead of relying on static rules, workflows were designed to respond dynamically based on real-time data. AI agents handled complex logic and continuously improved performance over time.

This shift enabled the team to move beyond automation and toward true operational intelligence.

The Impact

Workflows became more flexible, adaptive, and efficient.

The team was able to respond faster to changes and optimize processes without constant manual intervention.

  • More adaptive and intelligent workflows

  • Improved decision-making speed

  • Reduced manual adjustments

Conclusion

Speed-to-Lead redefined how their operations functioned by moving from static automation to intelligent systems.

Lake View Labs enabled a new level of flexibility and performance across their workflows.
